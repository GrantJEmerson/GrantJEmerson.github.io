Clipstrument

Published on Jul 3, 2020

A video clip powered instrument for Swift Playgrounds on iPad

Clipstrument is a musical instrument that allows for the playback, looping, and manipulation of short audio samples from animated films from the public domain in sync with their respective video clips. Clipstrument was built using the UIKit and AVFoundation frameworks.
