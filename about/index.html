My name is Grant Emerson, and I have been developing iOS apps for four years. I have personally published multiple apps to the App Store. I have extensive experience using Swift and a working knowledge of other programming languages such as Objective-C, C, C++, and Java.

Besides programming, my favorite activities are playing music on my bass guitar and making music using virtual instruments and a DAW. My passion for creating music has led me to explore how to develop apps for musicians. After publishing three music and audio apps to the App Store (Modulin, Dompel, and Plotony), I have acquired a considerable amount of experience working with Apple's audio frameworks such as Core Audio, Audio Unit, and AVFoundation. I hope to share some of my experience using these frameworks and Swift on this blog.
