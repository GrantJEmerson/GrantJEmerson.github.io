My name is Grant Emerson, and I have been developing apps for Apple's platforms since 2017. I have extensive experience with Swift, SwiftUI, and UIKit. I have personally published multiple apps to the App Store.

In Spring 2023, I graduated from the University of Central Florida with a B.S. in Computer Science and a Minor in Mathematics. I have accepted a return offer to rejoin Amazon as a software engineer working on the Flex app.
